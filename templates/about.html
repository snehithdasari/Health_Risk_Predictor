About VitalRisk Insight

Predictive analytics leveraging state-of-the-art machine learning models to assess personal health risks.

Our Technology

VitalRisk Insight utilizes an ensemble of advanced Machine Learning classifiers trained on extensive health records. By analyzing key lifestyle, demographic, and clinical parameters, the system correlates patterns to predict cardiovascular issues, hypertension, and diabetes with high accuracy.

Underlying Models

Random Forest

An ensemble learning method that constructs a multitude of decision trees, providing robustness and preventing overfitting.

XGBoost

Optimized distributed gradient boosting library highly efficient at handling complex, non-linear health data interactions.

Support Vector Machine

A powerful supervised learning model that finds the optimal hyperplane separating healthy vs at-risk clinical profiles in high-dimensional space.
